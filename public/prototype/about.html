About Us

Welcome to Bountiful Bicycle Center. We have been serving the greater Salt Lake and Davis County areas for nearly 50 years.

Our experienced, friendly, and knowledgeable staff are here to help you choose the perfect bike and accessories for your needs. We pride ourselves in having something for EVERYONE. Our professionals boasts over 60 years of combined experience in bicycle sales, service, fitting, riding, and racing. We feel that most bike brands and shops offer a good selection of bikes and products, but we'll go the extra mile for you in our customer service, in addition to offering a superior product.

If you're ever in the area please stop in at the shop or if you see us out riding say hi! If we don't already know your name we will soon.

Our History

Bountiful Bicycle center has been serving the needs of cycling enthusiasts since march 11th of 1960. Referred to often as the 'schwinn shop,' we have come a long ways since our early schwinn days.

As the shop has evolved from 1960 to today, it has had only 4 owners, we have been in our current location now for 7 years, and were located in our previous location, just 6 blocks south of where we are now, for 15 years.

We have and always will pride ourselves in offering the best customer service and the most diverse product selection available, outfitting kids from 2 years old to those kids and elite racers that seem to never have enough bikes and goodies.

Bountiful Bicycle would also like to thank our awesome customers for their dedicated patronage and support, you all rock! If you don't know us yet, or haven't been in lately, stop in and say hi, we look forward to many more years of relationships and success!
