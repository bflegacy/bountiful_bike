Club / Team

Bountiful Mazda Bountiful Bicycle Cycling Club

Joining the BMBBC club is easy! Stop by Bountiful Bicycle Center to sign up and get your new jersey. Club Dues are $30 per year (cash or check) and all Club members must purchase a Club Jersey ~$75.

Membership benefits include:

  • 4 group rides every week
  • Registration to Logan Century or $30 toward Little Red Ridding Hood Century
  • Private Summer Pool Party
  • Post LOTOJA dinner in Teton Village
  • Great friendships and camaraderie
  • Capoforma club Clothing at cost
  • Club discounts at Bountiful Bicycle Center
  • Weekly club specials at Bountiful Bicycle Center

Any question or for more information contact Jeff Warren info@bmbbc.com or talk to anyone at the shop.

More information can be found at the Official BMBBC Website.

Events

Spin Classes Here at the Shop

  • Classes are held Monday/Wednesday evenings 5:30-7pm, and Tuesday/Thursday mornings 5:30-7am.
  • Classes are being held NOW and will run until the mid-March.
  • There are 10 Lemond Revmaster Pro spin bikes and 1 Cycleops Fluid2 trainer available (you are more than welcome to bring your own bike and trainer for the class).
  • Bikes are spec'd with Pilot computers that read Speed, Cadence, Distance, and Heart Rate.
  • Gary's program will be a heart-rate based program, heart rate straps are required for it.
  • We will reserve spin bikes first come first serve, 2 business days in advance during business hours via shop phone.
  • If you have fit measurements, saddles, or specific pedals please provide those to us or bring them so your bike is setup the way you would like.
  • SPD's (mtn spd) pedals will be available for everyone who would like to use them on the spin bikes.
  • Classes cost $3 per class, 5 and 10 class punch passes will be available for purchase.
  • If you plan on attending the early morning Monday class please be here early enough to get setup and on the bike at 5:30 sharp so that we can close and lock the front doors.
