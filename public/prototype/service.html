Service

Check out the links below for pricing on our most common services. If you don't see what you're looking for on the list, fear not, we can service just about anything on any bike. Just give us a call

  • Standard Level

    Complimentary: Free

    We'll check over your bike's headset, gears, brakes & pads, rims, tubes, tires, cables, chain, grips, saddle, hubs, & bottom bracket. Then we will recommend the tune up or service your bike really needs.

  • Level I

    Basic Tune: $39.99

    We adjust hub, headset, bottom-bracket bearings, both brakes, and derailleurs. We also lube your chain.

  • Best Value!

    Level II

    Best Value: $54.99

    In addition to the Bronze Tune service we, true (straighten) both wheels, and re-lube all needed areas.

  • Level III

    Attention to Detail: $119.99

    In addition to the Silver Tune we replace all cables and housing on the bike.

  • Level IV

    Complete Overhaul: $219.99

    This tune includes everything from the Gold Tune and covers a complete disassembly, overhaul, inspection, and professional re-assembly
